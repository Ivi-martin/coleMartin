📝 Mini Test
📖 Understanding Can and Can't
✅
CAN
It can swim. (puede nadar)
❌
CAN'T
It can't fly. (no puede volar)
📋 Structure (Estructura):
✅ Affirmative: It can + verb → It can swim.
❌ Negative: It can't + verb → It can't fly.
❓ Question: Can it + verb? → Can it run?
✔️ Answer yes: Yes, it can.
✖️ Answer no: No, it can't.
